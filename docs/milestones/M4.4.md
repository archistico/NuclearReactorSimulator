# M4.4 — Condensate & Feedwater Train

## Status

**VALIDATED.**

M4.4 local build and complete automated test-suite validation were reported successful. M4.4 is the validated baseline for M4.5.

## Scope delivered

- canonical `CondensateFeedwaterSystemDefinition` over the validated M4.3 condenser/hotwell topology;
- exact one-train ownership of every legacy M3 feedwater seam;
- canonical hotwell → condensate pump → feedwater inventory → feedwater pump → steam-drum target topology validation;
- reuse of existing `PumpDefinition` / `PumpFlowSolver` physics without a second hydraulic integration path;
- mandatory zeroing of legacy M3 external feedwater mass sources while M4.4 owns condensate return;
- canonical feedwater-inventory fluid nodes with committed/candidate mass, energy, temperature and phase diagnostics;
- bounded manually commanded educational thermal-conditioning power with explicit positive external-energy accounting;
- deterministic condensate/feedwater pump diagnostics including flow, boost, losses and shaft-power demand;
- backward-compatible M4.3 supplemental-source-term composition preserving one thermofluid integration;
- closed-mass-path, legacy-boundary exclusion, thermal-conditioning accounting and determinism tests;
- ADR 0035 and `docs/CONDENSATE_FEEDWATER_TRAIN.md`.

## Explicitly deferred

- detailed heater/deaerator extraction-steam networks;
- automatic hotwell and steam-drum/feedwater level control;
- makeup-water chemistry and treatment;
- generator electromagnetic coupling and grid synchronization;
- integrated secondary-cycle reference heat-balance gate.

These belong to M4.5, M4.6 and later milestones.
