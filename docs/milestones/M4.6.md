# M4.6 — Integrated Secondary-Cycle Heat Balance

## Goal

Reconcile the complete manually commanded M3 + M4 secondary cycle and generator path under one deterministic mass/energy audit boundary before steady-state baseline work begins.

## Delivered

- `IntegratedSecondaryCycleDefinition` as the canonical top-level wrapper over the validated M4.5 generator/grid composition;
- `IntegratedSecondaryCycleInputs`, solver, step result and immutable snapshot without new mutable physical state;
- `SecondaryCycleHeatBalanceAudit` covering nuclear heat, pump hydraulic power, feedwater conditioning, condenser rejection, turbine shaft transfer, generator mechanical input, electrical export and generator losses;
- coupled thermofluid + rotor stored-energy reconciliation;
- complete reactor-to-grid first-law closure with explicit raw residuals;
- supplemental thermofluid power classification residual;
- explicit shaft-transfer and mechanical-to-electrical cross-domain reconciliation residuals;
- closed-loop external-mass and mass-closure diagnostics surfaced at the top-level snapshot;
- deterministic repeated-step coupled verification without hidden state correction;
- ADR 0037 and `docs/SECONDARY_CYCLE_HEAT_BALANCE.md`.

## Explicitly deferred

- configurable full-plant reference operating point;
- long-run full-plant drift gate;
- gross/net plant-efficiency baseline diagnostics;
- automatic governor, AVR, synchronizer or process control;
- protection/interlock logic.

Those belong to M4.7 and M5.

## Validation

Validated locally: build and complete automated test suite passed. M4.6 is the validated baseline for M4.7.
