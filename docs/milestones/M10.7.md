## Critical semantics

1. A save archive is replay evidence, not authoritative physical state.
2. Loading always reconstructs the exact embedded versioned scenario through `ScenarioSessionFactory` and verifies every archived fingerprint fail-closed.
3. Checkpoint restore replays only the exact archive prefix ending at the selected M9.1 checkpoint and verifies its fingerprint.
4. Operator actions and M10.5/M10.6 automation intents remain separate deterministic journals and are both restored at the validated `N → N+1` boundary.
5. Full recording is opt-in in the desktop to avoid hidden every-step overhead.
6. A loaded/restored verified session may resume the same deterministic recording prefix; no unrelated second trace is silently started.
