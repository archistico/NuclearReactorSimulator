# M7.6 — Power Manoeuvring & Normal Shutdown

**Status:** VALIDATED

## Scope

- exact-version `stable-low-load-parallel-operation` v1 handoff;
- bounded electrical-load raise/lower through canonical M4.5 requested-power inputs;
- coordinated reactor/turbine/electrical manoeuvring through existing M2/M5.3, M5.4 and M4.5 owners;
- observational temperature/void response checks with explicit quantitative-xenon unavailability;
- controlled normal-shutdown sequence: unload, breaker open, rod insertion, turbine rundown and post-shutdown main circulation.

## Acceptance

- initial session is paused, trip-free, breaker-closed and carrying a stable low electrical load;
- load raise/lower changes only canonical requested electrical power and measured output follows the validated generator/grid path;
- reactor power is changed only through rod-command/reacitivity/kinetics ownership and turbine speed only through the validated M5.4 governing seam;
- temperature/void checks remain observational and xenon is never synthesized outside the operational snapshot boundary;
- normal shutdown can unload/disconnect and begin controlled rod insertion while preserving main circulation;
- complete build and automated suite must pass locally before validation.

## Out of scope

Formal objective scoring, richer procedure-assistance/evaluation semantics and training-session assessment remain M7.7. Fault injection and abnormal/transient scenarios remain M8.
