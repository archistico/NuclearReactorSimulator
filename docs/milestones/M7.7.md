# M7.7 — Training Objectives, Procedure Guidance & Evaluation

**Status:** VALIDATED

## Scope

- deterministic accepted-operator-action journal at the scenario command boundary;
- per-fixed-step observational training checkpoints independent of UI publication cadence;
- generic scenario objective criteria and integer scoring over historical plant checkpoints and accepted operator actions;
- optional `Hidden`, `ChecklistOnly` and `Guided` assistance modes that never alter physics or scoring;
- explicit procedure-deviation penalties for emergency actions used as substitutes for the normal procedure;
- integrated 100-point normal-operations capstone over the validated M7.6 operating path.

## Acceptance

- training evaluation sees every deterministic simulation step even when presentation snapshots are sparsely published;
- rejected scenario commands and runtime-host controls do not enter the operator-action journal;
- changing guidance mode cannot change simulation inputs, logical stepping, checkpoint history or score;
- objective scoring is deterministic for the same initial condition and accepted command sequence;
- historical checkpoints remain achieved once first observed and retain their first logical-step index;
- emergency/protection actions remain physically available and authoritative below Application even when scored as procedural deviations;
- complete local build and automated suite passed on 2026-07-21; M7.7 is validated and the M7 gate is complete.

## Out of scope

Fault injection, fault scheduling, abnormal/transient scenario ownership and incident-specific evaluation remain M8. M7.7 does not add hidden randomness, automatic procedure execution or any second physical/control/protection state owner.
