## Status

VALIDATED — local build and complete automated test suite reported passing by the user.

## Delivered

- canonical `FeedwaterBoundaryDefinition` and `SteamExportBoundaryDefinition` mapped to existing steam-drum inventory/outlet nodes;
- `PrimaryCircuitBoundarySystemDefinition` requiring exactly one feedwater and one steam-export boundary per drum;
- complete canonical per-step `PrimaryCircuitBoundaryInputs`;
- controllable non-negative feedwater/export mass-flow inputs;
- explicit feedwater specific internal energy input;
- committed-state steam-export specific-energy removal;
- signed external mass-flow and power declarations in `PlantNetworkSourceTerms`;
- `PlantNetworkSourceTerms.Combine(...)` for staged M3 source-term composition;
- extended `PlantNetworkAudit` with declared external mass flow and balance mass-rate residual;
- immutable feedwater/export/system boundary snapshots;
- tests for topology validation, input completeness, signed external accounting, network mass/energy closure and composition with M3.6 separation;
- ADR 0030 and `docs/PRIMARY_CIRCUIT_BOUNDARIES.md`.

## Deliberately not included

- feedwater pump/heater/deaerator dynamics;
- turbine admission or steam-line transport;
- condenser/hotwell/condensate return;
- automatic drum-level or steam-pressure control;
- relief/safety valves;
- integrated long-run primary-circuit operating point, which remains M3.8.
