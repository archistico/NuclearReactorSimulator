# M5.4 — Turbine, Steam & Feedwater Control Loops

## Goal

Bind M5.1/M5.2 measured-signal controller primitives to canonical turbine-admission valves and condensate/feedwater pumps, while preserving M4 hydraulic, turbine, condenser and feedwater ownership.

## Delivered

- `TurbineSecondaryControlLoopDefinition` / `TurbineSecondaryControlSystemDefinition` semantic loop bindings;
- turbine-speed and generator-load admission-governor loop roles;
- source-drum steam-pressure admission loop role;
- steam-drum level to canonical feedwater-pump loop role;
- condenser hotwell-mass to canonical condensate-pump auxiliary loop role;
- new canonical `condenser/{id}/hotwell-mass` measured source;
- explicit exclusion of stop valves from normal governing;
- M4.2 stage-group mass flow derived from the limiting canonical stop/control/admission valve-path projection;
- no second hydraulic integration or duplicate valve/pump physical state;
- integrated M5.3 + M5.4 automatic-control composition over one measured frame and one M4.7 physical step;
- validation that M5.3/M5.4 actuator physical targets are disjoint;
- loop diagnostics for setpoint, measurement availability, controller output, saturation and physical target;
- ADR 0042 and secondary-cycle control documentation.

## Intentionally deferred

M5.5 owns permissives, interlocks, reactor SCRAM, turbine/generator trips and protection override arbitration. M5.6 owns alarm/annunciator presentation semantics.
