# M8.7 — Safety-Response Scenario Pack

**Status:** VALIDATED / HOTFIX 2 — M8 gate COMPLETE.

## Goal

Close the planned M8 scenario/fault phase with deterministic safety-response exercises that combine existing fault definitions, observational acceptance criteria, protection/control response verification and logical operator-action timelines without adding new physical ownership.

## Delivered

- `SafetyResponseScenarioPack` with three capstone exercises: protection fail-safe, large-break-class response and station-blackout-class response.
- Exact reuse of M8.3/M8.5/M8.6 fault declarations rather than duplicated or scenario-specific fault physics.
- `SafetyResponseCheckpointEvaluator` over committed `ControlRoomSnapshot` only.
- 100-point deterministic acceptance/scoring plans using M7.7 checkpoint/prerequisite semantics.
- `SafetyResponseEvaluationSession` joining current assessment with the existing accepted operator-action journal.
- Regression tests for pack composition, exact fault-type reuse and presentation-only checkpoint evaluation.
- ADR 0066 and `docs/SAFETY_RESPONSE_SCENARIO_PACK.md`.

## Non-goals

- no new physical fault types;
- no scripted trip, isolation, pressure, level, inventory or recovery outcomes;
- no new protection or controller owner;
- no wall-clock timestamps in evaluation/timeline state;
- no licensing-grade LOCA, station electrical distribution, ECCS, fuel-damage or severe-accident modeling.

## Validation gate

M8.5 hotfix 2, M8.6 and M8.7 hotfix 2 were validated together after a clean restore/build/test run. The M8 gate is complete and M8.7 hotfix 2 is the official baseline carried into M9.1.
