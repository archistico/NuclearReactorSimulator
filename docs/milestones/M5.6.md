# M5.6 — Alarms & Annunciator State

Status: **VALIDATED**

## Scope

- deterministic alarm conditions over canonical measured signals;
- observational alarm conditions over M5.5 protection functions/actions/interlocks;
- non-latching and latched-until-reset annunciator semantics;
- acknowledgement independent from physical protection reset;
- safe explicit alarm reset after acknowledgement;
- deterministic first-out grouping;
- monotonic logical event ordering;
- immutable alarm/event snapshots for later UI and recorder use.

## Acceptance criteria

1. Alarm acknowledgement never resets or changes M5.5 protection state.
2. Latched alarms remain annunciated after the initiating condition clears until explicit safe alarm reset.
3. Non-latching alarms clear automatically with their conditions.
4. First-out selection is deterministic and stable until reset.
5. Event sequences are monotonic and contain no wall-clock dependency.
6. Invalid measured-signal behavior is explicit per alarm definition and never falls back to perfect true state.
7. M5.6 composition runs the validated M5.5 protected physical step once; alarm processing is observational only.


## Validation

M5.6 was validated locally after the corrected baseline compiled and the complete automated test suite passed.
