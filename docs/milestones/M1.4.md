# M1.4 — Valves

## Status

Validated — local build and full test suite passed.

## Objective

Add controllable valve restrictions over the validated M1.3 passive pipe model without introducing a competing hydraulic solver.

## Delivered

- normalized strongly typed `ValvePosition`;
- normalized `ValveFlowCoefficient`;
- linear, quick-opening and normalized equal-percentage characteristics;
- immutable `ValveDefinition` and `ValveState`;
- explicit fail-closed, fail-open and hold-last-position behaviour;
- `ValveCharacteristicSolver`;
- `ValveFlowSolver` composed over `PipeFlowSolver`;
- exact fully-closed and fully-open endpoint semantics;
- conservative bidirectional mass/internal-energy transfer inherited from M1.3;
- deterministic fixed-step runtime integration tests;
- ADR 0011 and valve-model documentation.

## Acceptance criteria

- closed valves produce exactly zero mass and energy transfer despite non-zero pressure difference;
- fully-open valves reproduce the underlying fully-open pipe result exactly;
- partial opening modulates flow monotonically according to characteristic;
- pressure reversal remains bidirectional and upstream-energy aware;
- fail-safe actions resolve to deterministic effective positions;
- endpoint balances remain exactly conservative;
- identical inputs produce identical results;
- runtime pulse segmentation produces the same final snapshot;
- full solution builds with warnings as errors and all tests pass locally.
