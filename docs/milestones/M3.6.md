# M3.6 — Steam Drums, Separation & Recirculation

## Status

Validated through successful local restore/build/test execution reported by the user.

## Delivered

- backward-compatible explicit `ReturnCollectorNodeId` for main-circulation loops;
- canonical `SteamDrumDefinition` and `SteamDrumSystemDefinition` with exactly one drum per circulation loop;
- dedicated drum inventory and steam-outlet topology validation;
- `SteamDrumLevelFraction` diagnostic;
- committed-state ideal phase separation for subcooled liquid, saturated mixture and superheated vapor;
- saturated liquid/vapor energy routing through the existing simplified M1.7 water/steam properties;
- conservative internal `PlantNetworkSourceTerms` for drum -> steam outlet + liquid recirculation;
- immutable per-drum/system snapshots with inventory, phase, quality, void, level, flows and separation residuals;
- tests for topology, phase split, source-term closure and plant-network mass/energy audit integration.

## Deliberately not included

- feedwater or steam-export external boundaries (M3.7);
- detailed separator efficiency/moisture carryover;
- drum swell/shrink geometry;
- safety valves;
- turbine coupling.
