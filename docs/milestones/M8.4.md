# M8.4 — Turbine / Generator / Feedwater / Condenser Transients

**Status:** VALIDATED / HOTFIX 2

## Scope

- deterministic turbine-trip transient through the existing M5.5 manual-trip/protection latch and M4 steam/rotor owners;
- deterministic generator-trip/load-rejection transient through M5.5 generator-trip arbitration and M4.5 breaker/electromagnetic loading ownership;
- feedwater degradation/loss transient pack by reusing validated M8.2 hydraulic pump degradation/trip effects on the canonical `feedwater-pump`;
- condenser cooling degradation/loss through a temporary bounded overlay on canonical M4.3 `CondenserCoolingBoundaryInput.AvailableHeatRejectionPower`;
- a dedicated versioned `secondary-transient-ready / v1` initial condition that reuses the validated M7 recipe and adds finite condenser cooling capacity without creating new physical state;
- four explicit scenario definitions with deterministic logical-step triggers and no scripted physical outcomes.

## Acceptance

- turbine trip sets the existing canonical protection latch and lets existing stop/isolation/rotor physics determine the response;
- generator trip causes the existing generator protection path to open the breaker and reject electrical load; M8.4 never writes rotor torque or electrical output directly;
- feedwater degradation/trip acts only through M8.2 canonical pump constraints and the one M3/M4 plant-network integration boundary;
- condenser cooling degradation scales only the existing heat-rejection capacity; cooling loss sets that capacity to zero while condenser pressure/vacuum remain consequences of conserved exhaust inventory and M4.3 closure;
- fault clearance restores the persistent condenser cooling input but does not undo latched trips or restore prior plant state;
- unknown rotor, generator or cooling-boundary targets fail closed;
- scenario timing remains logical-step deterministic and independent of wall clock/UI publication cadence;
- local build and complete automated suite explicitly passed after hotfix 2; M8.4 is validated.

## Deliberate limits

M8.4 is a deterministic educational transient pack, not a detailed turbine blade-path, synchronous-machine electromagnetic-transient, feedwater-heater/deaerator or circulating-water-system simulator. Feedwater transients are limited to the canonical pumps and inventories already modeled. Condenser degradation changes available heat-rejection capacity only; it does not add detailed tube fouling, air ingress, ejector failure or circulating-water hydraulics. Larger leak/LOCA-class scenarios are introduced by the subsequent M8.5 candidate; electrical-loss/station-blackout-class scenarios remain M8.6.
